Getting Started with JambGenius

Welcome to JambGenius!

Follow these simple steps to start your JAMB preparation journey.

1

Create Your Account

Sign up using your email or Google account. Click the "Sign In" button on any page and choose "Sign up".

2

Complete Your Profile

Add your information, profile picture, and preferences. Click on "Profile" in the navigation after signing in.

3

Start with Practice Mode

Begin with free practice mode to familiarize yourself with the questions. Select any subject and start practicing at your own pace.

4

Take Daily Challenges

Complete daily challenges to earn points and stay consistent with your preparation.

5

Join the Community

Connect with other students in the Community Chat. Share tips, ask questions, and motivate each other.

6

Take Mock Exams

When you're ready, unlock the full Mock Exam Mode for ₦1,000 to experience the real JAMB CBT environment with 180 questions and 2-hour timer.

7

Track Your Progress

View your performance analytics to see your improvement over time and identify areas that need more attention.

Tips for Success

  • Practice consistently every day, even if just for 30 minutes
  • Review explanations for questions you get wrong
  • Focus on your weakest subjects first
  • Take at least one full mock exam before the real test
