Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 9, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to JambGenius. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our JAMB preparation platform.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and profile information when you create an account
  • Performance Data: Your practice scores, exam results, and learning progress
  • Payment Information: Transaction details when you purchase mock exam access (processed securely through Paystack)
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our platform, including pages visited and features used
  • Communication Data: Messages sent in the community chat and support inquiries

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our JAMB preparation services
  • Track your learning progress and provide personalized recommendations
  • Process payments for mock exam access
  • Send important updates about your account and our services
  • Respond to your support requests and questions
  • Ensure platform security and prevent fraud

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. Your account is secured with Firebase Authentication, and all payment processing is handled securely through Paystack. We never store your payment card details on our servers.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service Providers: Firebase (authentication), Paystack (payments), and other essential services
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt out of promotional communications
  • Download your data

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience, remember your preferences, and analyze platform usage. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

8. Children's Privacy

Our platform is designed for students preparing for JAMB. If you are under 18, please ensure you have parental consent before using our services.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by email or through a notice on our platform.
